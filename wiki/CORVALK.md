"Valkyrie" is a CORE aircraft unit included in *SCTATest*.
It is classified as a tech 1 air transport unit. It has no defined build description, and no categories to define common builders.

### Transport capacity
This unit has 4 small attach points, 2 medium attach points, and 1 large attach point. Using an attach point that isn't small costs a number of small attach points. Specifically 2 for medium, and 4 for large.
